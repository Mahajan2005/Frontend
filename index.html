What is a Zodiac ?

A zodiac is a belt-shaped region of the sky divided into 12 equal parts, each associated with a constellation. It is commonly used in astrology, where the 12 zodiac signs correspond to different personality traits based on a person’s birth date

Most caring Zodiac signs

  1. Cancer – Naturally nurturing, deeply empathetic, and protective of loved ones
  2. Pisces – Emotionally intuitive, selfless, and always ready to support others.
  3. Virgo– Shows care through acts of service and practical help.
